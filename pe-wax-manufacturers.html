The Product - PE Wax

We are a trusted PE Wax exporter in India that supplies premium quality wax. PE Wax is a refined wax made from low polymer waxes from petrochemicals industries by refining and distillation to remove the water and solvents and low molecular chains. It melts on heating to around 100 deg c and provides external lubrication in products like PVC pipes and profiles, masterbatches, road marking paints, and many other applications.

General Properties of MLA UNILUB PE Wax:

  • Low viscosity, high softening point, good toughness
  • Non-toxic, excellent thermostability, low volatility, and aids better pigment dispersion.
  • External lubricant and internal lubricant ( as per the formulation ) that can improve plastic processing production efficiency and appearance of finished products.
  • It can improve the strength of plastic products, promote the plasticization of PVC products, improve the lubricating effect, and increase the gloss of the surface.

Properties of good quality PE Waxes supplied by MLA Group:

  • A good PE wax should be crisp (non-oily) which is reflected in its penetration hardness.
  • A good PE wax for masterbatches or PVC with minimum oil content has hardness 4-6. As the hardness increases, it becomes more costly and is used in specialty applications and as it decreases it becomes more oily and sticky.
  • There are many unrefined or semi-refined PE waxes in the market which have hardness 8-20. Our PE wax colour is brilliant white.
  • As the average molecular chain length increases the wax becomes more viscous. Our wax generally has a viscosity below 50 cps, so its flow is high, which is beneficial for lubrication and is suitable for general masterbatches and PVC applications.

Applications Of PE Wax:

As a leading PE Wax manufacturers in the World, we produce superior quality PE Wax that can be widely used in the manufacture of masterbatch, granulation, plastic, steel, PVC pipe, hot melt adhesive, rubber, shoe polish, leather brightener, cable insulation material, floor wax, plastic profile, ink, injection molding, and other products.

Other uses Of PE Wax include:

  • Lubricant:
  • a Due to its excellent external lubrication effect and strong internal lubrication effect, it has good compatibility with polyethylene, polyvinyl chloride, polypropylene, and other resins, and can be used as a product in extrusion, calendering, and injection moulding.
  • b It can be used as a lubricating dispersant for filler masterbatch and degradation masterbatch, it can improve the processing performance, surface gloss, lubricity, and thermal stability of HDPE, PP, and PVC.
  • c It can also be used as a lubricant for cable insulation materials.
  • As an anti-slip agent, It can improve the gloss, durability, hardness, and abrasion resistance of paper, increase water resistance and chemical resistance, and increase the aesthetics of paper.
  • As a rubber processing aid, it can enhance the diffusion of fillers, increase the extrusion molding rate, increase mold flow, and facilitate demolding.
  • As a pigment carrier. It has good light resistance and chemical properties that can improve the abrasion resistance of paints and inks, improve the dispersion of pigments and fillers, prevent pigments from sinking, and can be used as a flattening agent for paints and inks
  • In addition, it can also be used to make shoe polish, candles, crayons, cosmetics, leather agents, hot melt adhesives, etc.

FAQs



How to identify good quality PE Wax?

There are many unrefined or semi-refined PE waxes in the market which have hardness 8-20. Our PE wax colour is brilliant white. When you touch it with your hands, the genuine product has good smoothness. It feels like plastic rather than wax. It is hard, brittle, fragile, and does not form a mass at room temperature. Paraffin-containing fakes are greasy, greasy to the touch, feel like touching candles, or rough, look like unreconciled dough pieces, soft and sticky. If you hold them with your hands, they will form a ball. The genuine product smells like plastic, while the fake product smells of paraffin wax or a strong peculiar smell.


Why should I purchase PE Wax from MLA Group?

As a leading manufacturer and supplier of PE Wax, we only manufacture premium quality products at leading market prices. Our PE wax is brilliant white in color and generally has a viscosity below 100 cps, majorly below 50 cps, so its flow is high, which is beneficial for lubrication and is suitable for general masterbatche, PVC pipes, profiles, compounds and most other applications.


Is UNILUB PE Wax equivalent to Clariant / Honeywell / BASF grades?

Ours is a refined PE wax which is much cheaper than the above speciality waxes which are made by chemical polymerization and cost almost double. However our waxes replace some of their grades successfully, where melting point and viscosity requirement is not very different from our specifications. Oxidized PE waxes are also different from our product.
