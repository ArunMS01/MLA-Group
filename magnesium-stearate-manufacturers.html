The Product - Magnesium Stearate

Magnesium Stearate is a magnesium salt of stearic acid. It is an additive widely used by the pharmacological area because it provides various functionalities to the formulations since it has lubricating, water-repellent, anti-flake, and release properties which attribute it to the products that are made with it. It is also used in plastics as a lubricant.

Magnesium Stearate Properties

  • It is a light white powder with a fine appearance.
  • It feels smooth and greasy.
  • It is insoluble in water, ethanol, and ether.
  • It is soluble in hot water and hot ethanol.
  • It decomposes into stearic acid and the corresponding magnesium salt when acidic.

Magnesium Stearate Application

We are a leading Magnesium Stearate supplier and exporters of Magnesium Stearate that is widely used in :

  • Food, medicine, paint, plastics, rubber, textiles, and other industries, mainly as an emulsifier, mold release agent, lubricant, stabilizer, accelerator, cosmetic base material, etc.
  • This substance partially replaces the white matt pigment, which also makes it possible to use it in cosmetology. In creams, it acts as a lubricant and significantly increases glide.
  • Magnesium stearate is also used as an active stabilizer in PVC production.
  • In addition, the substance is used as a supplement in the manufacture of pharmaceuticals.

How should magnesium stearate be stored?

It is recommended to store the magnesium stearate in a dry, ventilated area. You should also avoid direct sunlight and high temperatures. You can store in a closed container; in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area, away from strong oxidizing agents.
