The Product - Calcium Stearate

Calcium stearate, also known as calcium salt and octadecanoic acid, is an odorless white powder used in many manufacturing processes and preparations. It is a carboxylate created by heating stearic acid and calcium oxide under controlled conditions.

At MLA Group, we manufacture calcium stearate for a wide range of industrial processes. Our products are manufactured in SS 316 L plants, suitable for various plastics, industrial, food and pharmaceutical applications.

Uses Of Calcium Stearate

One of the leading Calcium Stearate supplier in India, we supply a product that is of excellent performance and reliable quality that is widely used in food, medicine, cosmetics, plastic, and rubber industries, mainly as lubricants, emulsifiers, stabilizers, mold release agents, accelerators, cosmetic base materials, etc.

It is mainly used for application in the following areas:

  • As a waterproofing agent, lubricant, and plastic auxiliary.
  • It can also be used for non-toxic soft films such as food packaging and medical appliances.
  • As a heat stabilizer for polyvinyl chloride, a lubricant for a variety of plastic processing, and a mold release agent.
  • As a halogen absorbent in polyethylene and polypropylene, it can eliminate the adverse effects of residual catalyst in the resin on the color and stability of the resin.
  • As a plasticizer in rubber processing, it can soften natural rubber and synthetic rubber, but has almost no effect on vulcanization.
  • In the production of pencil lead, medicine, cosmetics and perfume industry.
