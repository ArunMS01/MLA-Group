The Product - PVC Heat Stabilizers

We provide PVC Heat Stabilizers (one packs - lead based & calcium-zinc non-toxic) in powder and flakes form. In PVC Processing (extrusion, injection molding, etc.), the polymer is subjected to a high temperature which can lead to burning (degradation or breaking of polymer chains). Heat stabilizers are lead compounds that are added to prevent burning by capturing and inactivating the labile chlorine that is produced on heating of PVC.

Conventionally, a combination of three chemicals, TBLS, DBLS, and LS (Tribasic lead sulphate, dibasic Lead stearate, and Lead stearate) were used as heat stabilizers along with Calcium Stearate as a co-stabilizer. MLA Group is the leading manufacture of these single stabilizers in the highest purity and performance.

Nowadays, oneâ€“pack stabilizer is used in place of the three chemicals as a heat stabilizer. It is made by in-situ reaction of lead oxide with stearic acid along with many other components like waxes, antioxidants, inorganic stabilizers etc, which were not there in the conventional system of single stabilizers above.

Its performance depends on two things:

  • the lead oxide content which provides stabilization by deactivating the chloride radical formed during heated melt processing of PVC during extrusion and
  • by providing lubrication so that the residence time inside the screw extruder (heated zone) is reduced, so less deterioration is there.

MLA Group produces a dozen of popular one-pack grades with different lead contents as well as over a hundred customized grades.

APPLICATION OF PVC STABILIZERS

Tribasic lead sulphate, Dibasic lead stearate, Lead stearate are used as heat stabilizers while processing PVC items so that they do not get burnt/degraded due to high temperatures used in processing.

Our one packs are a combination of above and other additives such as anti-oxidants etc., all put together in one product which provides ease of handling while its function is the same as above: to provide heat stability to PVC.

There are several benefits of using our one pack PVC Heat Stabilizers that include: reduced labour cost, reduced handling losses, reduced inventory, a better homogenous mixture from processing. What is the preferred dosage of PVC Heat Stabilizers?

The dosage of stabilizers per hundred parts of resin ( PVC ) is 2.5-3.5 phr in pipes and 3.0 to 12.0 phr in different types of cables, but it depends on lead content in stabilizer and the filler used by PVC product manufacturer. If the filler is more, the dosage required is more. Detailed application knowledge and formula/recipe for any customer can be provided by our technical services managers.
