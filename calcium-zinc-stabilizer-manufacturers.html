The Product - Calcium Zinc Stabilizer

Calcium zinc stabilizers are a complex mixture of over 10-15 ingredients like stearates, organic stabilizers, inorganic stabilizers, antioxidants, lubricants, fillers, etc. It is a non-toxic stabilizer that is the preferred choice over lead-based stabilizers which are completely banned in developed countries and slowly being phased out of the world.

Uses Of Calcium Zinc Stabilizer

MLA Industries is a leading exporter of a wide range of Calcium Zinc Stabilizer extensively used for Pipes, Profiles, Cable, Footwear, etc, manufactured using only the finest materials conforming to stringent quality norms.

Our Calcium Zinc Stabilizer is used in :

  • In the cable industry or plasticized PVC ( 40-50 phr of plasticizers like DOP, DBP, TOTM, CPW, etc ) the grades are developed to give a good:
  • Static stability (color of PVC compound at 180C for different times, Tikki test, a chart of color deterioration with time is prepared )
  • Thermal stability ( congo-red time or time taken for the PVC compound to decompose )
  • Volume resistivity, the wet VR etc ( electrical properties ) Special grades for HVAC and HVDC cables
  • Color holding ( uncured compound should not change color with ageing ).

For this, we have a full-fledged PVC lab to check the performance of our various grades. The grades are defined on the basis of the service temperature of the cable and can be 70, 90, 105, or 125 degrees C. We accordingly have grades CZ-815, CZ-835, CZ-55, CZ-75, CZ-889, and many others as per the specific requirement of clients.

In the pipe industry, which is unplasticized, hard, or rigid. The stabilizer is developed to give good stability and smooth flow through the extruder. This is done by static heat stability test, Rheometer test to check flow and stability properties, and through checking the load in actual extrusion on a pilot or full-scale plant. We have many grades including CZ-RP40 against Sunace 911 and CZ-321-P against more recent grade 3210 of Baerlocher. We also have economy grades like RP-20 for WPC and other applications like marble flooring.

FAQs



What is the use of calcium zinc stabilizers?

The use of calcium zinc stabilizers has been common in PVC. They are widely used in many flexible and rigid PVC applications because of the characteristics of calcium zinc stabilized materials. Such stabilizing systems give products that have a high degree of clarity, good mechanical and electrical properties, excellent organoleptic properties, and good outdoor weather ability.

Are calcium zinc stabilizers safe to use?
