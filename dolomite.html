MICRONIZED ULTRAWHITE NATURAL CALCIUM CARBONATE

Microcal is an ultrawhite micronized natural Calcium Carbonate, for achieving best results at higher loadings in various applications. The product has exceptionally high whiteness and free flowing characteristic and facilitates enhanced processing characteristics at higher loadings.

The product is manufactured from high purity mineral of limestone family. The particle structure is spherical and microcrystalline. The product is micronized in latest grinding equipment based on German technology. The product is available in various particle sizes depending on customers requirement.

QUALITY ASSURANCE SYSTEM

To offer better quality products the quality is assured at following levels -

Minning >> Manual sorting to ensure maximum whiteness in lumps.
>> Chemical analysis at mines before despatch to works.
Micronizing >> Qualified engineers and production personnel to ensure consistency of processing parameters.
>> Fully equipped analytical laboratory at each works for -
    ---Particle size analysis.
    ---Tests for physical properties like Bulk density, Oil absorption, Brightness etc.
