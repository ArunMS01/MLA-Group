Ethylene Bis Stearamide manufacturers in India - Our Product

Ethylene Bis Stearamide or EBS by MLA Group has low acid value ( free fatty acid ), high melting point, and excellent white colour, and high purity. Used as a high melting lubricant for engineering plastics, masterbatches, filler compounds, rubber, etc, EBS is compatible with most waxes and plasticizers. The polarity of its two central amide groups and the non-polarity of the two fatty chains make this product an excellent surface-active agent which is incompatible with virtually all solvents and most polymer systems. Ethylene Bis Stearamide Uses

Ethylene Bis Stearamide is an activator for various plastics and synthetic resins. It is a release agent, a pigment dispersant, a lubricant, a surface gloss agent, and an activator for rubber products, coating, Ink additives, etc. In India, we are a leading name among Ethylene Bis Stearamide suppliers.

Uses

As a lubricant

EBS has good internal and external lubricity, mold release and smoothness, can accelerate melting, reduce melt viscosity, save processing energy consumption, increase product toughness, extend mold life, and give products a good appearance. Mainly used for PVC.PE.PP.PS.ABS resin, but also for phenolic and amino plastics.

As a general-purpose dispersant

EBS is used as a general-purpose dispersant for plastics and is widely used in PVC products. It can also be used in combination with other lubricants and has a very significant synergistic effect. In the rubber industry, it is used as an anti-blocking agent and release agent, as well as a hard rubber surface treatment agent.
